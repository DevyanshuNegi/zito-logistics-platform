## Phase 3 Strategy: The 10x Lever

**Phase 3 = Market Model Shift** (Months 7-10)

**Current:** ZITO owns the supply (drivers + vehicles)  
**Phase 3:** ZITO becomes aggregator (commission-based)

### New Player: Transporter Marketplace
- Small transport companies join ZITO
- They bring their own drivers + fleet
- ZITO takes 8-10% commission
- ZITO becomes like Uber (network + commission)

### Why This Scales 10x:
- No vehicle/driver acquisition cost
- Capital-light model
- Multi-sided platform benefits
- Network effects (more transporters → more capacity → more shippers)

### Phase 3 Revenue Streams
- Commission on transporter bookings: KES 150M/month
- Load board (driver bidding): KES 80M/month
- Fleet partner SaaS: KES 50M/month
- Agency recruiting commission: KES 30M/month

**Phase 3 Target:** KES 400M+/month (ACHIEVES VISION)

### Why Phase 3 Transporter Marketplace Wins

**Competitors' Approach:**

- Network effects (more supply → more demand → more supply)
