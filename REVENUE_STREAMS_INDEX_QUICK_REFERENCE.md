## 🔑 Critical Success Factors (Tier 1 Priority)

1. **Recurring Billing Framework** (Must ship Q1)
   - Blocks: Driver tiers, pickup-drop, fleet SaaS
   - Impact: 35% of Year 1 revenue

2. **Fintech Partner Agreement** (Must sign Q1/Q2)
   - Blocks: Fast payout, microfinance, invoice financing
   - Impact: 15% of Year 1 revenue

3. **Partner Recruitment** (Q1-Q3)
   - Fuel: 50+ stations = KES 750M potential
   - Mechanics: 20+ networks = KES 100M potential
   - Clearing agents: 10+ agents = KES 240M potential
   - Impact: 20% of Year 1 revenue

4. **Data Quality for ML** (Q2)
   - Pricing model training
   - Maintenance prediction model
   - Impact: 5% of Year 1 revenue (but enables future growth)

5. **Regulatory Compliance** (Q2-Q3)
   - Cross-border: KRA/Customs clearance
   - Insurance: ICRA approval
   - Microfinance: CBK guidelines
   - Impact: Blocks 30% of Year 1 revenue if delayed
